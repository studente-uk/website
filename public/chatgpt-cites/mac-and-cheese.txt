```
# Homemade Macaroni and Cheese Recipe

## Ingredients

- 1 pound elbow macaroni
- 1/4 cup unsalted butter
- 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
- 2 cups whole milk
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- 1 teaspoon ground mustard
- 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- 4 cups shredded cheddar cheese
- 1/2 cup breadcrumbs

## Instructions

1. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.

2. Cook the macaroni according to the package instructions until it's al dente. Drain it and set it aside.

3. In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.

4. Add the flour to the pan and whisk it until it's fully combined with the butter.

5. Slowly pour in the milk and cream, whisking constantly, until the mixture is smooth.

6. Add the garlic powder, onion powder, ground mustard, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper to the pan. Whisk everything together until the spices are fully incorporated.

7. Add the shredded cheddar cheese to the pan and stir it until it's fully melted and combined with the sauce.

8. Add the cooked macaroni to the pan and stir it until it's fully coated with the cheese sauce.

9. Transfer the mac and cheese to a large baking dish.

10. Sprinkle the breadcrumbs over the top of the mac and cheese.

11. Bake the dish in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the breadcrumbs are golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.

12. Remove the dish from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Enjoy your delicious homemade macaroni and cheese!
```
